Maintenance Protocol

MECHANIX PRO
CERTIFIED.

Every MARC8 vehicle undergoes our proprietary 11-point certification. Not a checklist — a standard of excellence.

01

11-Point Inspection

Comprehensive pre-deployment checks covering engine, brakes, tyres, electricals, interiors, and all safety systems.

02

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled oil changes, filter replacements, and fluid top-ups aligned to OEM standards — never deferred.

03

Digital Repair Logs

Every service event recorded digitally. Full history accessible to asset owners with timestamped audit trails.

04

Hygiene Standards

Cabin deep-clean after every trip cycle. Interior sanitization, upholstery care, and odour elimination protocols.

05

Audit Certifications

Third-party periodic audits validate our standards. Certification documents provided to corporate clients on request.

06

Zero-Tolerance Policy

Any vehicle failing a single inspection point is immediately pulled from the active fleet until cleared by certified technicians.
