Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

At TaqwaSteps, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information — Name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Location Data — Your approximate location to calculate accurate prayer times (Azan & Iqamah) for your area
  • Usage Data — Prayer tracking history, Dhikr counts, streak data, and Tahajjud reflections
  • Device Information — Device type, operating system, and notification permissions
  • Payment Information — For Premium subscriptions, payments are processed securely through Google Play. We do not store your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide accurate prayer times based on your location
  • To send Azan and Iqamah notifications
  • To track your Salah, Dhikr, and Tahajjud streaks
  • To store your private Tahajjud reflections securely
  • To send app updates and launch notifications (only if you signed up)
  • To process Premium subscription payments
  • To improve the app experience

3. Tahajjud Reflections — Private Data

Your Tahajjud reflections are completely private. They are:

  • Encrypted and stored securely
  • Never shared with anyone — including us
  • Only accessible by you on your device
  • Never used for advertising or analytics

4. Location Data

We use your location only to calculate prayer times. We do not:

  • Track your movements
  • Share your location with third parties
  • Store your location history

You can disable location access in your device settings. Prayer times can also be set manually.

5. Push Notifications

We send notifications for:

  • Azan time reminders (5 minutes before)
  • Iqamah time alerts
  • Morning and Evening Adhkar reminders
  • Post-Salah Dhikr reminders
  • Tahajjud alarm

You can manage notification preferences in the app settings or your device settings at any time.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Google Play — for payment processing (Premium)
  • Firebase — for secure data storage and authentication
  • When required by law

7. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Your data is protected using industry-standard encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

TaqwaSteps is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of email notifications
  • Request a copy of your data
