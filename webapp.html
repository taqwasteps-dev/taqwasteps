📜 THE PROPHET ﷺ ON SALAH
مُرُوا أَوْلَادَكُمْ بِالصَّلَاةِ وَهُمْ أَبْنَاءُ سَبْعِ سِنِينَ، وَاضْرِبُوهُمْ عَلَيْهَا وَهُمْ أَبْنَاءُ عَشْرٍ
"Command your children to pray when they are seven years old, and discipline them for it when they are ten."
ABU DAWUD · 495
الصَّلَاةُ عِمَادُ الدِّينِ
"Prayer is the pillar of religion. Whoever establishes it has established religion; whoever abandons it has destroyed religion."
BAYHAQI · SHUAB AL-IMAN
📖 QURAN ON SALAH
إِنَّ الصَّلَاةَ كَانَتْ عَلَى الْمُؤْمِنِينَ كِتَابًا مَّوْقُوتًا
"Indeed, prayer has been decreed upon the believers a decree of specified times."
SURAH AN-NISA · 4:103
وَأَقِيمُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَآتُوا الزَّكَاةَ وَارْكَعُوا مَعَ الرَّاكِعِينَ
"And establish prayer and give zakah and bow with those who bow."
SURAH AL-BAQARAH · 2:43
✨ BENEFITS OF SALAH
🛡️Salah protects from shameful and harmful deeds — it is a direct shield from Shaytan (Quran 29:45)
💆5 prayers a day wash away sins like a river at your door — narrated by the Prophet ﷺ (Bukhari)
🌟It is the first deed accounted for on the Day of Judgement — and if it is sound, all else follows
🤲Salah is a direct conversation with Allah — the closest a servant can be to his Lord is in Sujood
🕰️It disciplines the soul and instills tawakkul — building consistency, patience and gratitude daily
